Heritage

A Colonial Legacy of Sport

Established in 1916 during the British era, the Patna Golf Club stands as a bastion of elegance and sporting excellence in the heart of Bihar. Spread across vast green acres adjacent to the Governor's House, it is one of the oldest and most prestigious golf clubs in North India.

The club has hosted numerous national and regional tournaments and continues to be a favorite getaway for the city's elite, offering world-class facilities amidst serene, manicured landscapes.

Offerings

World-Class Facilities

  • 1
    Professional Standard 6,200-Yard Course

    A beautifully maintained 18-hole course with challenging bunkers, water hazards, and strategically placed greens that test every skill level.

  • 2
    Heritage Clubhouse with Luxury Dining

    A colonial-era clubhouse exuding old-world charm with premium dining, a bar, and lounges for post-game relaxation.

  • 3
    Regular National & Regional Tournaments

    Hosts prestigious events including the Bihar State Championship and invitational corporate tournaments throughout the year.

  • 4
    Golfing Academy for New Players

    Professional coaching programs for beginners and juniors with certified PGI trainers and modern training equipment.

  • 5
    Driving Range & Practice Greens

    Dedicated driving range and multi-tier practice areas for perfecting your swing and short game.

  • 6
    Pro Shop & Equipment Rental

    Fully stocked pro shop with top golf brands and rental services for clubs, carts, and accessories.

  • 7
    Swimming Pool & Fitness Center

    Complementary wellness facilities including a temperature-controlled pool and a fully equipped gym for members.

  • 8
    Event Hosting & Private Functions

    Elegant outdoor lawns and club halls perfect for corporate events, weddings, and social gatherings.

Visitor Information

Membership

Club membership available for residents. Guest passes for non-members on weekdays. Corporate memberships also offered.

Location

Near Raj Bhavan (Governor's House), Bailey Road. 3.5 km from Patna Junction. Best reached by cab or personal vehicle.

Pro Tips

Early morning tee-offs are perfect for the mild Bihar weather. Don't miss the Sunday brunch at the clubhouse — it's legendary!
