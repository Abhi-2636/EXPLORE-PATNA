About

The Ultimate Splash Zone

Hungama World is Central Bihar's most exciting water park, situated on the scenic Bihta Road just outside Patna. Spread over 12 acres, it offers a world of aquatic entertainment designed for all ages — from toddlers to thrill-seeking adults.

With state-of-the-art filtration systems, certified safety equipment, and trained lifeguards, Hungama World ensures every splash is worry-free. It's the perfect weekend escape from the Bihar heat!

Attractions

Thrill Rides & Zones

  • 1
    Mega Wave Pool

    Experience the ocean in the heart of Bihar! A massive wave generator creates up to 4-foot waves for an exhilarating surf experience.

  • 2
    Multi-Lane Racing Slides

    Race your friends down 60-foot speed slides with multiple lanes — the fastest splash wins bragging rights!

  • 3
    Rain Dance Arena

    Dance under artificial rain with pulsating music, LED lights, and DJ sessions every weekend — the ultimate party zone.

  • 4
    Lazy River Float

    Relax on inflatable tubes and drift along the 600-meter lazy river circuit through tropical landscaping.

  • 5
    Kids' Aqua Playground

    A dedicated safe zone with mushroom showers, mini slides, splash buckets, and shallow pools for children under 10.

  • 6
    Tornado Tunnel Slide

    Plunge into a dark enclosed spiral slide with surprise twists and a thrilling free-fall splash at the end.

  • 7
    Private Cabanas & Lounge

    Premium cabanas with sun loungers, personal service, and shade — perfect for families wanting a relaxed VIP experience.

  • 8
    Food Court & Snack Bars

    Multiple dining options with local and continental food, fresh juice bars, and ice cream parlors across the park.

Visitor Information

Timings

Open 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM. Weekend hours extended to 7:00 PM. Closed on Tuesdays for maintenance.

How to Reach

Located on Bihta Road, about 20 km from Patna Junction. Best accessed by cab or personal vehicle. Ample parking available.

Pro Tips
