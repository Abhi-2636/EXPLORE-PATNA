About

A Gateway to the Cosmos

The Indira Gandhi Planetarium, popularly known as Taramandal, is a dome-shaped astronomical observatory located in the heart of Patna. Inaugurated on March 1, 1993, it stands as one of the finest centers for space science education in Asia.

Named after India's former Prime Minister, the planetarium uses state-of-the-art Opto-mechanical star projectors and Digital Full-Dome systems to deliver immersive journeys through stars, galaxies, and the fabric of spacetime.

Experiences

Key Wonders

  • 1
    High-Resolution Digital Sky Projection

    Experience the night sky in stunning detail with 8K resolution full-dome projections that map thousands of celestial objects.

  • 2
    Interactive Science Exhibition Galleries

    Walk through hands-on exhibits covering physics, astronomy, and space exploration with touchscreen kiosks and working models.

  • 3
    Astronomy Workshops for Students

    Regular workshops and lectures by ISRO scientists to inspire the next generation of space explorers from Bihar.

  • 4
    Regular Evening Sky-Watching Sessions

    Join telescope sessions during astronomical events — eclipses, meteor showers, and planetary alignments under expert guidance.

  • 5
    Multi-Language Sky Shows

    Enjoy cosmic shows narrated in Hindi, English, and regional languages with captivating visual storytelling.

  • 6
    Solar System Model Park

    An outdoor scale model of the solar system for educational walks and photo opportunities amidst lush landscaping.

  • 7
    Space Museum & Artifacts

    Replica rockets, satellite models, and meteorite samples from ISRO and international space agencies on permanent display.

  • 8
    3D Astronomy Film Screenings

    Immersive 3D films about black holes, the Big Bang, and interstellar travel in the dedicated 3D theater section.

Plan Your Visit

Visitor Information

Timings

Open 10:00 AM – 5:30 PM, closed on Mondays and national holidays. Shows run every 1.5 hours.

How to Reach

Located near Indira Gandhi Science Complex, Bailey Road. Just 4 km from Patna Junction. Auto, cab & bus available.

Pro Tips

Book sky shows early — seats fill up fast on weekends. Best to visit weekday mornings for a calm, crowd-free experience.
